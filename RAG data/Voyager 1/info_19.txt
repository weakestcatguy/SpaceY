Far future
Provided Voyager 1 does not collide with anything and is not retrieved, it is expected to reach the theorized Oort cloud in about 300 years[124][125] and take about 30,000 years to pass through it.[81][92]
Though it is not heading towards any particular star, in about 40,000 years, it will pass within 1.6 light-years (0.49 parsecs; 100,000 astronomical units) of the star Gliese 445, which is in the constellation Camelopardalis and 17.1 light-years from Earth (as of 2010).[126] That star is generally moving towards the Solar System at about 119 km/s (430,000 km/h; 270,000 mph)
